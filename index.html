Welcome to WildeCoreOS

Hi, on this site you will find releases of WildeCoreOS. This OS is based on LineageOS with customizations from RisingOS. The wallpapers are from Pinterest.

All releases will be available on the Downloads tab. Updates and betas will also appear here. Please be patient, as releases may take longer due to hardware limitations.

Supported devices in the first release:

  • Moto G84
  • Moto G54
  • Moto Edge 30 and 20 series
  • Moto Edge 40 Fusion
  • Moto Edge 50 Fusion
